What is Vocaloid?

Vocaloid is a vocal synthesizer software developed by Yamaha. Music producers can use Vocaloid to create vocals for their songs. There are many different Vocaloids, each with a unique voice--Vocaloids can sound childish, mature, cutesy, mellow, and more. Voice banks are often marketed with anime-esque character designs representing the vocal style. The most popular Vocaloid is Hatsune Miku, developed by Crypton Future Media, who has skyrocketed in fame since her release in 2007.
