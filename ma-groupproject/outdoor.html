Best outdoor activities in the Greater Seattle area

The PNW is known for the incredible beauty of the outdoors. These are the top 5 things to do if you come to the greater Seattle area and are looking for an outdoor adventure.

Mountain Biking

The Pacific Northwest is known for a wide variety of riding. In rain and shine there is something for the Seattle area to provide when it comes to Mountain Bikes.

Galbraith

Nestled in the picturesque town of Bellingham, a mere two-hour drive from the bustling city of Seattle, Galbraith emerges as a haven for biking enthusiasts. This outdoor utopia boasts an extensive network of trails, stretching over an impressive 65 miles. Each trail is a thrilling adventure waiting to unfold, adorned with an abundance of jumps, berms, and technical features that would leave any rider exhilarated and challenged. For those new to the world of mountain biking, gentle, winding paths wind through the forest, providing a welcoming environment to develop and hone basic skills. These trails offer a scenic journey through the natural beauty of Galbraith, allowing beginners to build confidence while enjoying the tranquil surroundings. The true thrill-seekers and experienced riders will find their match on advanced trails that push the limits of skill and courage. Galbraith's challenging descents, daring jumps, and intricate technical sections provide the ultimate playground for those seeking an adrenaline rush and an opportunity to showcase their skill.

Tiger mountain

Tiger Mountain, located just outside Seattle, is a mecca for mountain biking enthusiasts. Renowned for its diverse trail network and stunning natural beauty, Tiger Mountain offers an exhilarating and challenging experience for riders of various skill levels. One of the key highlights of Tiger Mountain is its extensive trail system, featuring a variety of routes that cater to everyone from beginners to advanced riders. Novice cyclists can ease into the sport on well-maintained and beginner-friendly trails, allowing them to build confidence while surrounded by the serene beauty of the mountainous terrain. For the seasoned experts seeking an extra dose of adrenaline, Tiger Mountain delivers with advanced and expert-level trails. These trails are designed to push the limits of skill and courage, featuring steep descents, challenging rock gardens, and jumps that provide a thrilling challenge for even the most experienced riders. Beyond the trails, Tiger Mountain offers breathtaking panoramic views of the Cascade Range, adding a scenic backdrop to every ride. The Pacific Northwest's signature evergreen forests surround the trails, creating a serene and immersive experience that enhances the joy of mountain biking.
