Shopping

Favorite shopping spots from locals

Bellevue Mall

The mall itself is a modern and stylish structure, designed to provide visitors with a sophisticated shopping experience. It features a diverse range of stores, including renowned fashion brands, specialty boutiques, electronics retailers, and upscale department stores. Some of the notable brands and stores that were present as of my last update included Nordstrom, Macy's, Apple, and other well-known fashion and lifestyle brands.

University Village

The shopping center is designed in an open-air format, featuring a pedestrian-friendly layout with walkways, beautiful landscaping, and plenty of seating areas. It is home to a variety of well-known national retailers, upscale boutiques, specialty stores, and local businesses. Some of the notable stores that were present as of my last update included high-end fashion brands, home decor stores, technology retailers, and other specialty shops.

Pike Place

The market is famous for its wide array of fresh produce, locally sourced food products, and specialty goods, including seafood, fruits, vegetables, flowers, artisanal crafts, and handmade goods. It is home to a variety of independent vendors, farmers, artisans, and craftsmen, who bring a unique and eclectic blend of products to the market.
